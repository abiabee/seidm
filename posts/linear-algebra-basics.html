Linear algebra is the language of modern data. We start with a vector v ∈ ℝn, then build to matrices that transform those vectors. A matrix A applies a linear map: y = A·x.

Spans and Bases

The span of vectors is every combination you can reach. Choose a basis to coordinate your space — then a matrix is just a basis-aware machine.

🚩 Tip: a matrix column is where the basis vector goes.

From Math to ML

In ML, weight matrices transform features. Training nudges entries of A so predictions match targets. Gradients flow; matrices learn.
